Our Mission

ETS has been providing quality tutoring to residents of the Palos Verdes Peninsula and neighboring South Bay for over twenty years. Our tutoring staff is made up of credentialed teachers and tutors with advanced degrees and/or specializations. All of our tutoring sessions are 1 on 1 and take place in individualized rooms. We also offer in-home and in-library tutoring. Students are matched with tutors based on each student's individual needs.


ETS seeks to instill the spirit of achievement in all of our students through mentorship, innovation, and student engagement.
